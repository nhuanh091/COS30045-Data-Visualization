Energy Efficient Televisions

Understanding Energy Ratings

Televisions in Australia display an Energy Rating Label. Higher star ratings generally indicate lower electricity consumption during typical household use.

What Affects Power Usage?

Screen size, brightness settings, and display technology (LED, OLED, QLED) all influence energy consumption.
